What is FontCraft?

FontCraft is a free Unicode font generator that lets you convert regular text into 50+ fancy styles — bold, cursive, fraktur, glitch, bubble, and more. No login, no watermarks, completely free.

How Does It Work?

We use the Unicode Mathematical Alphanumeric Symbols block (U+1D400–U+1D7FF) to map standard letters to their styled equivalents. Because these are real Unicode characters — not images or custom fonts — they paste and display correctly on every platform: Instagram, TikTok, Discord, Twitter, YouTube, WhatsApp, and more.

Why We Built This

We noticed that most font generators were cluttered with ads, required signups, or had slow interfaces. FontCraft is designed to be instant — you type, it converts, you copy. That's it.

Our Mission

To give every creator access to beautiful typography, for free, forever. Whether you're designing an Instagram bio, naming a Discord server, or creating a game username — FontCraft has you covered.
