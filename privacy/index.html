Last updated: January 2025

Overview

FontCraft ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. FontCraft is a static website — all font conversions happen in your browser. We never see what you type.

Data We Collect

Automatically Collected

Like all websites, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may log basic access data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps. This is standard server logging and is outside our control.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics (such as Google Analytics with IP anonymisation enabled) to understand general usage patterns — which pages are popular, which countries visitors come from, and similar aggregate data. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Contact Form

If you submit our contact form, your name, email address, and message are sent via Formspree. Please review Formspree's privacy policy for details on how they handle form data.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not store the text you enter into the font generator — it never leaves your browser.
  • We do not require registration or login.
  • We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

Cookies

FontCraft itself sets no cookies. Third-party services (analytics, embedded fonts from Google Fonts) may set their own cookies. You can block these via your browser settings.

External Links

Our site may link to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and recommend reviewing their policies independently.

Children's Privacy

FontCraft is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
