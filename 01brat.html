Make Your Own Brat Album Cover

Welcome to the ultimate Brat Font Generator. Inspired by Charli XCX's iconic "Brat" album aesthetic, this tool helps you create custom artwork featuring the signature lime green background and low-fidelity Arial typography that defined a summer of club culture.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Enter Text: Use the text area to type your message. All text is automatically converted to lowercase to match the "Brat" style.
  • Style Effects: Choose Blur for the classic hazy look, Scribble for an underground punk vibe, or Clean for high-contrast sharpness.
  • Download: Click the download button to export a high-resolution 1080x1080 square image, perfect for profile pictures or social media posts.

Why is the "Brat" Look so Popular?

The "Brat" aesthetic is a masterclass in intentional lo-fi design. By using a standard web font (Arial) on a high-visibility lime green background (#8ace00), the design rejects traditional polish in favor of raw, immediate energy. It's accessible, instantly recognizable, and perfectly suited for the meme era.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the hex code for Brat Green?

The specific lime green used is #8ace00. Our generator uses this as the default background color.

What font does Charli XCX use on Brat?

The album art uses a version of Arial Narrow Italic (or simply Arial Bold with specific tracking). Our generator replicates this using a bold sans-serif stack to ensure it looks right on all devices.

Is this tool free?

Yes! This generator is completely free for personal use, allowing fans to join the "Brat Summer" movement with their own custom text.
